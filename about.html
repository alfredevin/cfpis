About Us

Miraculous Medal Clinic is a healthcare facility located in Boac, Marinduque, committed to delivering compassionate, reliable, and patient-centered medical services. The clinic provides essential health services such as Ob-Gyn check-ups, ultrasounds, general consultations, and Pap smear tests to the local community.

To improve the quality and efficiency of its services, the clinic has implemented the HealthCheck: A Customer-Focused Patient Information System. This digital platform simplifies the management of patient records, appointment scheduling, consultation tracking, and vital signs monitoring.

Through this innovation, Miraculous Medal Clinic aims to enhance the overall healthcare experience by offering a system that supports both medical professionals and patients through secure, organized, and accessible information.
