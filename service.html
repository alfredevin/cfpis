Services Offered

Ready for a check-up? Just click on your preferred service and easily schedule your appointment in a few steps.

A. Ob-Gyn Check-up

Routine gynecological check-ups are offered to assess reproductive health, detect any irregularities early, and provide guidance on women’s health concerns such as menstrual issues, family planning, and pregnancy care.

B. Ob-Gyn Ultrasound

This imaging service helps monitor pregnancy development and diagnose gynecological conditions. It provides clear internal visuals for accurate and safe assessments.

C. General Check-up

General health assessments are conducted to evaluate overall wellness, monitor vital signs, and provide early detection of potential health issues. Recommended for preventive care.

D. Pap Smear

A Pap smear is a preventive procedure that screens for cervical cancer and detects abnormal cell changes in the cervix. It’s a vital part of women's health and is recommended regularly.
